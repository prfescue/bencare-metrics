Channel Service Level Objectives (SLOs)

SLOs are team-level metrics monitored daily. They reflect how quickly and consistently the team responds across each channel. Individual ICs are not scored directly against SLOs — SLO performance reflects team-wide capacity and scheduling effectiveness.

IC Scorecard — Core Pillar Metrics

These three metrics define whether an IC is meeting expectations. Tier 1 and Tier 2 share the same targets — with one exception: tNPS is lower for Tier 2 to reflect the nature of more complex, escalated interactions.

IC Scorecard — Coaching Metrics

Coaching metrics are signals, not hard pass/fail thresholds. Sustained gaps trigger formal coaching plans. These apply equally across Tier 1 and Tier 2.
